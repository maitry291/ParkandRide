# ParkandRide

<!-- <a href="" class="button big">Download apk</a> -->

-Parking in developed and populated urban areas is often a point of concern and quite time-consuming. Park and ride service is a
technology-driven system that will help to solve this problem. 

-The main objective here is to facilitate parking lots where
private transport users park their own vehicles and
commute via public transportation mode in a hassle-free way. 

-As a result,usage of public transport increases, traffic congestion and
air pollution decrease, mobility around a city improves
and the commuters can reach their destinations in a
stress-free way. 

 
 ### üì± Features
 
-User's current location is tracked.

-User can contact customer support for their queries and provide feedback for their booking experience as well to make improvement.

-User can book available slot by making online payment.
